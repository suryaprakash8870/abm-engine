# ABM Engine — Demo Cheat-Sheet

One page. Keep it beside you. Each stop has one line to say and a fallback if you get stuck.

---

## Start (say first)

"This is the ABM Engine — eleven engines that take us from *who should we sell to?* all the way to *here's exactly who to contact, when, and why* — then learn from every closed deal. Let me walk it end to end."

## Rescue line (works anywhere you blank)

"Each engine does one job, owns its own data, and hands off over an event bus. A lot of what you're seeing runs on demo data until we connect the live keys — the logic is all real."

## Click path

ICP → Scoring → Target Accounts → Contacts → Signals → Awareness → Campaigns → Data Sources → Analytics

---

## 1. ICP  —  /icp

**Say:** "The Ideal Customer Profile — built from a wizard or learned from our closed deals. Everything downstream targets this."

**If stuck:** "It's versioned — every Refine cuts a new version, and the flywheel feeds wins back into it."

## 2. Scoring  —  /scoring

**Say:** "Every account gets a 0–100 fit score and a tier. These sliders are the weights — drag one, re-run, watch accounts re-tier."

**If stuck:** "Override lets a human force an account's tier — the human always beats the formula."

*(Between ICP and here: TAM sources every matching company and Enrichment AI-qualifies them — automatically, behind the scenes.)*

## 3. Target Accounts  —  /tal

**Say:** "The official target list. Finalize snapshots a version; Suppress pulls out accounts we shouldn't chase — existing customers, closed-lost."

**If stuck:** "You don't suppress everything — just the few exceptions; the rest stay in by default."

## 4. Contacts  —  /contacts

**Say:** "Now the people. It finds the buying committee per account and sorts them — decision maker, champion, influencer — with verified emails."

**If stuck:** "Sourcing runs as a background job so nothing blocks — Apollo finds the people, we verify the emails and map the roles."

## 5. Signals  —  /signals

**Say:** "The always-on radar. Website visits, webhooks and web research become weighted buying signals. Run web research — that's real AI extracting signals live."

**If stuck:** "First-party visits count far more than third-party noise, and old signals decay over time."

## 6. Awareness  —  /awareness

**Say:** "All those signals roll into one readiness score and a 5-stage funnel — Identified to Selecting. The 7-day arrow shows who's heating up now."

**If stuck:** "These routing rules are the trigger — score ≥ 60 → alert + task — that's what fires the campaigns."

## 7. Campaigns  —  /plays

**Say:** "When an account crosses the line, the right play fires automatically — alert the rep, draft the outreach, create the task. Mark the outcome right here."

**If stuck:** "Each play is picked by tier × stage, and the AI drafts the email — click Draft to show it."

## 8. Data Sources  —  /integrations

**Say:** "The only engine that touches your CRM. Everything writes back to HubSpot here — and closed deals read back in. Every write is logged."

**If stuck:** "This is also the switch — paste a key and an engine flips from demo data to live, no redeploy."

## 9. Analytics  —  /insights

**Say:** "The flywheel. Win-rate by tier proves the targeting works; it learns which signals predict wins and feeds that back to sharpen the ICP."

**If stuck:** "Click Refine your ICP — that's the loop closing back to Engine 01. It compounds the more you sell."

---

## Close (say last)

"That's the full loop — define, find, qualify, engage, act, and learn. One app, eleven engines, deployed and running today. The pieces marked partial are real engines waiting on live data keys — that's the Phase-1 work."

## If asked something you don't know

"Great question — let me confirm the exact detail and follow up." Never invent a number live.
